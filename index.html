The Game 'M.A.S.H.'

M.A.S.H. is a popular paper-and-pencil children's game for predicting the future. The name is an acronym of Mansion, Apartment, Shack, and House.

In my version of M.A.S.H., a player enters three inputs in six separate categories. Each category is assigned a random number, and a JavaScript for loop styles each input according to whether it matches its category's number.

There's no way to cheat—JavaScript's Math.random doesn't care what you want!

Baking Conversion Tool

After moving to Norway, I struggled to convert U.S. recipe measurements from cups into grams and mL. The tools I found online were often spammy and inaccurate, so I decided to make my own.

After determining the average density of baking flour, granulated sugar, unsweetened cocoa powder, and cornstarch, I wrote two functions that convert the user's input into their desired unit (g or mL).
